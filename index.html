ABOUT ME

Biography

Hello and welcome to my world! I am a Polish artist who have been active in Sweden for a long time. Now I have based myself in the Netherlands to continue with my work, study and explore the art world outside of Scandinavia. My heritage is Polish but I would say that Sweden has become lika a home for me, that’s the place where I grew up and got the opportunity to open up my creative side. I will always be grateful for that. I have been studying woodwork, ceramics and now I started my studies at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie in Amsterdam to get to know me and expand my artistic side.

My Work

Here you can see a beginning of a way in to my mind. It is a colorful world of organic forms in different medias and exploring techniques. The idea of my work is to make a mind interact with the work and forget the surroundings around one. Here you can find all of my different works. Feel free to explore and don’t hesitate to write to me if you have any comments or are interested.
